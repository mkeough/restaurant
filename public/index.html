Our Story

Pepe's pizza was founded in 1951 by Andrew Pepe. Originally born in Italy, Andrew and his family moved to America when he was 10. Andrew grew up in New York City. He always used to make pizza with his grandmother growing up, which is where he got his secret recipe from. Here at Pepe's, we use only the finest local ingredients for our pizza. We strive for our pizza to live up to the standards of Grandmo Pepe. Our pizza is the best around and satisfaction is guaranteed.
